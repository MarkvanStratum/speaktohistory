Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Speak To History, we value the privacy of your intellectual and historical explorations. This Privacy Policy details how OFFERPULSE LIMITED (Hong Kong) manages your personal information.

1. Data Collection

We collect essential information to provide our AI services:

  • Account Information: Email and password for authentication.
  • Historical Dialogues: We save your chat history with historical figures so you can continue your learning journey.
  • Usage Data: Basic technical logs (IP, browser type) to maintain site security.

2. Use of Information

Your data is utilized to:

  • Deliver and refine the AI chat experience.
  • Protect your account and our digital archive.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the accuracy of historical simulations.

3. Dialogue Privacy

We treat your conversations as personal educational records. OFFERPULSE LIMITED does not sell or share your chat logs with third parties for marketing purposes. Data processed by our AI partners is governed by strict security protocols.

4. Cookies

We use functional cookies strictly to maintain your logged-in state and ensure a seamless experience within the digital archive.

5. Secure Payments

All financial contributions for access to historical figures are handled by secure third-party processors. We do not store credit card information on our servers.

6. International Storage

As a Hong Kong-based entity, your data may be stored and processed globally. By using our service, you acknowledge and consent to this international data flow.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time via your account settings or our support channels.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our support team at OFFERPULSE LIMITED.
